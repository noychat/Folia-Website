OUR SERVICE

Only sourcing the finest leaves, Folia provides bespoke tea collections for elevated dining experiences. Our in-house sommeliers work with our partners to deliver distinct offerings for your business.

We recognize that a lot of individuals are unhappy with the quality and selection of tea at restaurants. Our goal is to create a curated tea experience to deliver to restaurants, enhancing their customer's tea drinking experience.

OFFERS

At Folia, we provide curated tea lists including the following essential items: a selection of fine tea leaves paired with multiple tea strainers. Our product makes sourcing and serving high end tea simple for restaurants while having luxury packaging and high quality loose leaf tea. We target businesses and restaurants whose customers are unhappy with the current selection or quality of tea offerings.

Folia Tea provides luxury, loose leaf tea. In comparison, our competitors present their tea in tea bags which decreases the enjoyability of the taste and experience of drinking tea. We will provide this service directly to your establishment, weather it be a restaurant, hotel, or any other business that would benefit from our tea selections.
