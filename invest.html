Help us to keep Folia brewing!

Our ask:

We seek $20,000 in funding from a VC investor to cover multiple sectors of our company such as labor costs, warehousing, product development, and marketing costs.


Where are the funds going into?

In the beginning stages of building our company, our labor cost will be somewhat low because founders will not be taking pay. Since the founders will not be taking on a salary initially all labor costs would go to the tea manufacturers for the workers who make our tea cans.

Other places where a large source of our funding would go towards is product development. We will need to understand what type of packaging keeps the tea the freshest, while also being able to hold the extra tea tools such as strainers. The final area we will be putting more effort into is our marketing strategy. We want to build a network of restaurants that will recommend us to others and stay faithful to our brand.


Where we are right now:

Referencing the P/L ratio, total revenue, and income statements of our competitors (Art of Tea, Tazo), we have currently valued Folia at 150K. We seek 20K. In 5 years, we project our growth to value Folia at over 1M.

Funding, at our current stage, will help support buying products, hiring employees, and covering other capital expenses.


Our future:

To create a competitive advantage over other beverage companies in our space we have created a comprehensive plan for how we will use our funds to grow the company at an exponential rate. In the end, we believe that 20k in capital will help us in many different areas of our business.

The first thing we intend to do with our capital is build out our sourcing and manufacturing process. Currently we are sourcing the tea wholesale than packaging each and every tea product by hand. We eventually want to offload this process to focus more on the marketing and administrative aspects of the company. Also by outsourcing this work to 3rd parties we will be able to make more of our tea at a quicker rate than previously done.

We also plan to set aside 15-20% of the funds for an emergency occurrences that may happen during the operation of Folia Tea so we can continue running the company. Our team also plans to put another 5-10% to the side to pay for business insurance and any other insurances we may need. This is crucial to our company.
