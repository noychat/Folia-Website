The Market

There is a large tea market in Los Angeles, where we are starting. The beverage industry in the US is worth $146 billion, and tea is rising as a large segment of that. We plan to target upscale Los Angeles restaurants whose customers are unhappy with the current selection or quality of tea offerings. In this high-end service marketplace, high-quality is crucial to satisfy our customers, which is why we will focus our efforts on premium tea sourced from the best select locations, but also amazing customer service, to best follow through with our customers throughout the whole process of receiving their currated tea.







Major Competitors

As of the moment, our major competitors include other caffeinated beverage distributors and higher-end prepared drinks (e.g. companies like Art of Tea, TeaVivre, Grocery Teas). Indirectly, we compete with drinks at upscale restaurants and hotels, such as spirits, wine, and other liquors. We expect to also be in line with premium restaurants such as Nobu and Sugarfish, as well as smaller restaurants like Moreton Fig, Town and Gown, and Tommy Club.
