What is Folia?

Only sourcing the finest leaves, Folia provides bespoke tea collections for elevated dining experiences. Our in-house sommeliers work with our partners to deliver distinct offerings for your business.

We recognize that a lot of individuals are unhappy with the quality and selection of tea at restaurants. Our goal is to create a curated tea experience to deliver to restaurants, enhancing their customer's tea drinking experience.

How do we do this?

We specifically focus on creating loose leaf teas that not only taste amazing but are also quick and easy to make. Not only do we offer teas, but also an extensive guide/app to our customers that explain how to prepare the tea correctly.

We source our teas from distributors that source from China, Taiwan and other areas. As we scale, we aim to establish direct relationships with tea gardens and buy our inventory straight from them. In terms of our branding, we will package our tea in our luxury packaging that also emphasizes the streamlined nature of our preparation and serving process.

Folia is aiming to obtain clients in Los Angeles and the USC community. We want to create events to educate people about luxury tea and promote our product to the community. With this, we can engage tea lovers and bring people together.

We also have carefully crafted a brand image around Folia, positioning us as a premium product that restaurants can trust. As such, our relationships and client lists are a valuable asset in ensuring our long-term success.
